Superconducting resonator qubits and electron-on-helium qubits

<trapping-title>Trapping Technique</trapping-title>

1. <trapping-notes>Superconducting resonators: microwave photons confined in circuit cavities</trapping-notes>
1. <trapping-notes>Resonator geometry determines photon mode frequencies</trapping-notes>
1. <trapping-notes>Electron-on-helium: electrons trapped above liquid helium surface</trapping-notes>
1. <trapping-notes>Image charge from helium surface creates attractive potential</trapping-notes>
1. <trapping-notes>Electrostatic gates control electron position and energy levels</trapping-notes>
1. <trapping-notes>Extremely clean system with minimal decoherence sources</trapping-notes>
